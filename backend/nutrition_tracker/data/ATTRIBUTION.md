# indb_nutrients.csv — attribution

Derived from the Indian Nutrient Databank (INDB), a subset of columns
(food name + energy/protein/carb/fat/fibre/free-sugar/sodium per 100g)
extracted from `INDB.xlsx` ("Nutrient Data" sheet) for use as a local,
no-API-key nutrition provider.

Source dataset: https://github.com/lindsayjaacks/Indian-Nutrient-Databank-INDB-

Citation:
Vijayakumar A, Dubasi HB, Awasthi A, Jaacks LM. "Development of an Indian
Food Composition Database." Current Developments in Nutrition. 2024;
8(7):103790. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cdnut.2024.103790

Note: the INDB itself was built in part from the ICMR-NIN Indian Food
Composition Tables (IFCT) 2017/2004, which are separately copyrighted by
the National Institute of Nutrition and are NOT redistributed here in raw
form — only INDB's own openly-licensed derived recipe/nutrient values are
included in indb_nutrients.csv.
